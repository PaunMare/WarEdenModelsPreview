THE GAME

WarEden is an Epic Fantasy Play&Earn PC Game designed for competitive hardcore gamers that enjoy playing skill-bassed MMORPG battlegrounds.

GAME OVERVIEW

ABOUT WAREDEN

  • 10 vs 10 battleground with Capture the flag mechanics
  • Deep RPG progression around 10 different classes
  • Tab-Targeting combat designed to push competitive skill-based plays to its limits
  • Epic Fantasy World with recognizable hand painted Art Style

All NFTs are only cosmetics that will shape the identity creation in WarEden metaverse. The value creation of NFTs is coming from within the world. Our aim is to make people to Keep the NFTs, not to primary to Sell. Players progres through skill and time consuming.

  • WarEden has been built on our custom-made engine, framework and tooling system where the team has complete control without paying the massive technical costs of third-party engines
  • Main component of the engine is the live code reload without using scripting language which gives us very fast iteration times and reduces overall development time

Immutable X is a layer 2 scaling solution on Ethereum with zero gas fees. Our collection is available on their platform.
