ABOUT WAREDEN

WarEden is an Epic Fantasy Play&Earn PC Game designed for competitive hardcore gamers that enjoy playing skill-based MMORPG battlegrounds.

  • 10 vs 10 battleground with Capture the flag mechanics
  • Deep RPG progression around 10 different classes
  • Tab-Targeting combat designed to push competitive skill-based plays to its limits
  • Epic Fantasy World with recognizable hand painted Art Style
