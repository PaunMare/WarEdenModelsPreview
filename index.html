THE GAME

WarEden is an Epic Fantasy Play&Earn PC Game designed for competitive hardcore gamers that enjoy playing skill-based MMORPG battlegrounds.

GAME OVERVIEW

ABOUT WAREDEN

  • 10 vs 10 battleground with Capture the flag mechanics
  • Deep RPG progression with numerus different classes
  • Tab-Targeting combat that is designed to push competitive skill-based plays to their limits
  • An Epic Fantasy World with a recognizable hand-painted Art Style

All NFTs are only cosmetics that will shape the identity creation in the WarEden metaverse. The value in the creation of NFTs is derived from within the world. We aim to encourage people to keep their NFTs, and not primarily have them to be Sold. Players progress through skill and time investment.

  • WarEden is built in our custom-made engine, framework, and tooling system where the team has complete control without paying the massive technical costs of third-party engines
  • The main component of the engine is the live code reload without the use of a scripting language, in turn, this gives us swift iteration times and reduces overall development time

Immutable X is a layer 2 scaling solution on Ethereum with zero gas fees. Our collection is available on their platform.
